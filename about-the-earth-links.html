The Earth Links - Our Story

Bridging Worlds, One Journey at a Time

Contact Us

At The Earth Links, we believe that the world has much more to offer than mainstream destinations. Our mission is to connect curious travellers with the beauty and richness of lesser-known countries, offering a glimpse into regions that are often overlooked.

The idea for The Earth Links was born out of a passion for discovery and a desire to showcase the warm hospitality and rich cultures of places like Afghanistan, North Korea, and beyond. Our goal is simple: to bring worlds together through travel, breaking down barriers and creating unforgettable experiences.

Why We Started This Venture

The Vision

The vision for The Earth Links came from a realization: there are so many wonderful, untapped destinations where the local communities are eager to welcome travellers with open arms. Countries like Afghanistan, often misunderstood, are home to some of the friendliest, most generous people you’ll ever meet.

We wanted to change the narrative, offering adventurous travelers the chance to experience these places firsthand, while ensuring their safety and comfort. Through carefully curated packages, we aim to provide authentic, life-changing experiences that go beyond typical tourism.

What Makes Us Different: Authenticity, Safety, and Connection

What sets us apart from traditional travel agencies is our focus on creating immersive, authentic experiences in countries that many travelers have never considered. From strolling through the markets of Kabul to exploring ancient landmarks in Pyongyang, our itineraries are designed to go beyond the usual.

Safety is always our top priority, and we work closely with local guides, authorities, and communities to ensure a smooth, secure journey. Most importantly, we believe that travel should connect people, and every experience we offer is about fostering human connections across cultures.
