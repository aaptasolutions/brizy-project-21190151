What types of products can I source through Xporters?

We source a wide range of products including artisanal crafts, food products, textiles, and more from emerging markets across the globe.

How does the custom product request service work?

If you cant find the product youre looking for, simply submit a custom request, and well leverage our network to source it directly from the relevant country.

Are the products listed verified for quality?

Yes, we take steps to verify the quality of products listed by exporters. However, we recommend that buyers perform their own due diligence, especially for large or specialized orders.

Can I list my business as an exporter on the platform?

Yes, businesses looking to export their products can list on Xporters after completing a registration and verification process to ensure compliance with international trade laws.

What countries can I source products from?

We focus on sourcing products from emerging markets in countries such as Afghanistan, North Korea, and others. If youre looking for a product from a specific country, let us know, and well do our best to assist.

How does payment and shipping work for international exports?

Payments are processed through secure payment gateways, and shipping options will be provided once the product is ready for export. Shipping costs and delivery times will vary based on the product and destination.

What destinations do you offer through Xplorers?

We specialize in offering travel packages to unique and less-traveled destinations such as Afghanistan, North Korea, and other regions often overlooked by mainstream tourism.

How does the custom travel package work?

Simply submit your travel preferences, and well tailor a package that suits your needs, including flights, accommodations, guided tours, and transportation, all while ensuring your safety and comfort.

Is it safe to travel to destinations like Afghanistan or North Korea?

We prioritize your safety above all else. We work with trusted local guides and authorities to ensure a secure and seamless experience for all our travelers.

What kind of support can I expect during my trip?

We offer concierge services and 24/7 support throughout your journey. From helping with travel adjustments to offering emergency assistance, were with you every step of the way.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, but cancellations or modifications are subject to our cancellation policy and any terms set by third-party providers such as airlines or hotels. Contact us for more information on your specific booking.

Whats included in the cost of my custom travel package?

Our packages generally include flights, accommodation, guided tours, local transportation, and select meals. Additional services like visas, insurance, and special excursions may incur extra fees.

What types of jobs are available through Xchange?

We offer a variety of roles, including teaching positions, technical roles, and specialized jobs in various sectors, with a focus on countries in emerging markets.

How does the custom job request process work?

If youre looking for a specific type of job, you can submit a custom request detailing your qualifications and preferences, and well work to find a matching opportunity for you.

Are the jobs legitimate and safe?

We vet all employers and job listings to ensure legitimacy and safety. However, we recommend that you also perform your own research and due diligence before accepting any offer.

Can I apply for jobs in multiple countries?

Yes, you can apply for jobs across multiple countries listed on our platform. We will assist you in navigating the application process for each specific region.

Do I need to speak the local language to apply for jobs?

Language requirements depend on the role and the country. Some positions, particularly teaching or technical jobs, may require proficiency in English or another specified language.

How do I get paid for jobs found through Xchange?

Payment terms are agreed upon between you and the employer. Xchange acts as a facilitator and does not handle payroll directly. We recommend clarifying payment schedules and terms with the employer before accepting a job.
