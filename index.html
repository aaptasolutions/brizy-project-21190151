Mission IMPOSSIBLE

True explorers see possibilities where others see fear!

Every nation aspires to be an economic superpower. However, due to geopolitical clashes or religious extremism, they often end up fighting battles for someone else's vision or dream. These wars, driven by a few hundred policymakers and governing authorities, leave millions in despair for decades.


It's easy to fantasize about ticking off bucket list items like visiting the 7 wonders or traveling to first-world countries, blowing all our savings, and posting pictures of an elite lifestyle. Fortunately, many of us have the privilege to do so, or will one day work towards achieving it. But imagine the lives of those born in so-called "dangerous countries."

While skydiving over the Palm in Dubai might be on our bucket list, a girl in an Islamic state might dream of simply receiving a basic education. While we might sail in Ibiza and dance at boat parties, someone in Sudan dreams of expressing themselves freely. As we relax on Bali's beautiful beaches, others face severe punishments for even basic interactions with the opposite sex. The comparisons are endless, but the point is, if you have the privilege to read this article, you are fortunate.


At Earthlinks, we strive to provide a gateway to some of the world’s least visited and most misunderstood countries. Every place has abundant talent, rich culture, history, and unique heritage. Unfortunately, much of it goes unnoticed and untapped due to unstable political scenarios, ongoing civil wars, and enduring humanitarian crises. A famous Hindu scripture says "Vasudaiva Kutumbakam," which means "the world is one family." Let's join hands and uplift our brothers and sisters to make the world a better place.
